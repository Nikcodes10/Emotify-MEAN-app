About

Emotify is a platform for users all around the globe to improve their emotional state of being. Through a systamized process and by providing insights, we aim to make it easy for you adapt with your mood swings.

Purpose

We at Emotify intend to build a community where people support each other during difficult times. In addition, we aim to bring the light of hope to those engulfed by the seemingly overpowering nights of despair.

Vision

A community where people are not afraid of speaking up on their personal issues.
A community where people understand each other's feelings.
A community where everyone is treated without any prejudice.

Contents

Emotify presents to you special as well as handpicked articles from all over the web as an attempt to lift you to glory. This venture aims at highlighting the importance of mental health in leading successful lives.
